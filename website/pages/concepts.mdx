## Social Model of Disability

The [social model of disability](https://www.inclusionlondon.org.uk/about-us/disability-in-london/social-model/the-social-model-of-disability-and-the-cultural-model-of-deafness/) has been developed by disabled people.
It views inaccessibility and discrimination for people with impairments as a result of societal ordering.
People with impairments are not inherently disabled, they are disabled by societal barriers.
Removing these barriers gives people with impairments fairer access and dignity.
Research software can benefit from barrier-removal to give as many researchers, and developers, access as possible.
